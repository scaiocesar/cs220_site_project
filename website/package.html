Packages to Brazil 2024

Iguazu Falls – Starting at $ 999 per person Day 1: Arrival in Foz do Iguaçu Arrival at Foz do Iguaçu International Airport. Transfer to your hotel. Free time to rest or explore the city. Day 2: Visit to Iguazu Falls - Brazilian Side Breakfast at the hotel. Full-day excursion to Iguazu National Park on the Brazilian side of the falls. Enjoy breathtaking panoramic views of the falls and the surrounding rainforest. Day 3: Iguazu Falls - Argentine Side Breakfast at the hotel. Cross the border into Argentina to explore Iguazu Falls from the Argentine side. Follow the extensive network of walkways to get up close to the falls and enjoy a boat ride under the cascades (optional). Day 4: Itaipu Dam Tour Breakfast at the hotel. Visit the incredible Itaipu Dam, one of the largest hydroelectric dams in the world. Learn about its engineering and environmental significance during a guided tour. Day 5: Bird Park and Wildlife Experience Breakfast at the hotel. Explore the Bird Park (Parque das Aves) to get up close to a variety of exotic birds and other wildlife. Afternoon at leisure for relaxation or optional activities. Day 6: Departure Breakfast at the hotel. Depending on your departure time, you may have some free time for last-minute shopping or exploration. Transfer to Foz do Iguaçu International Airport for your onward journey.

Rio de Janeiro Carnival – Starting at $ 1999 per person Day 1: Arrival in Rio de Janeiro Arrive at Rio de Janeiro International Airport. Transfer to your centrally located hotel in Rio. Enjoy your first evening exploring the city and soaking in the lively Carnival atmosphere in the streets. Day 2: Samba Parade - Access Group Breakfast at the hotel. Get ready to experience the magic of Carnival at Rio's Sambadrome. Attend the Samba parade of the Access Group, where samba schools compete for a spot in the Special Group. Day 3: Rio de Janeiro City Tour Breakfast at the hotel. Enjoy a guided city tour, visiting Rio de Janeiro's most iconic landmarks, including Christ the Redeemer, Sugarloaf Mountain, and the charming neighborhood of Santa Teresa. In the evening, return to the Sambadrome to witness more parades. Day 4: Street Carnival Block Party Breakfast at the hotel. Join one of the vibrant street Carnival block parties in Rio de Janeiro. Experience the energy and fun of the celebration in the streets. Day 5: Samba Parade - Special Group Breakfast at the hotel. Today is the main event: the Samba parade of the Special Group at the Sambadrome in Rio. Witness the extravagance, dazzling costumes, and infectious music of the most renowned samba schools. Day 6: Farewell Day Breakfast at the hotel. Enjoy your final hours in Rio de Janeiro. You can explore the city at your own pace, relax on the beaches, or do some souvenir shopping. Transfer to Rio de Janeiro International Airport for your return flight.
